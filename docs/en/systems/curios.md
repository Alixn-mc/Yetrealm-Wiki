# Gear and Curios

Yetrealm uses gear stages and Curios accessories to extend long-term survival progression.

## Gear Stages

| Stage | Core material | Upgrade template | Recipe count | Role |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Meteorite | Meteorite Ingot (yetrealm:meteorite_ingot) | Meteorite Upgrade Smithing Template (yetrealm:meteorite_upgrade_smithing_template) | 10 | The first structured Yetrealm gear stage after vanilla late game progression. |
| Ancient Metal | Bronze Ingot (yetrealm:ancient_metal) | Bronze Upgrade Smithing Template (yetrealm:ancient_metal_upgrade_smithing_template) | 10 | The second gear stage after Ancient Universe exploration, bridging ruins, ancient materials, and boss preparation. |
