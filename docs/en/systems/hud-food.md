# HUD, Food, and Experience

Yetrealm adds several survival-facing systems that affect day-to-day play beyond bosses and dimensions.

## HUD

The client HUD surfaces extended survival values such as armor, health, hunger, experience value, and extra movement resources depending on configuration.

## Food

Food diminishing returns and expanded hunger-related logic encourage varied food use in longer worlds. Some items permanently increase maximum health or hunger within defined limits.

## Experience

The mechanics guide notes that anvil and enchanting flows can use raw experience points and remove the vanilla "too expensive" limit in the relevant Yetrealm ruleset.
