# Status Effects and Attributes

Yetrealm combines vanilla, Apothic Attributes, and custom attributes to support boss combat, Curios builds, and world difficulty scaling.

## Common Attribute Areas

| Area | Examples |
| --- | --- |
| Combat damage | attack damage, attack speed, projectile damage, critical chance |
| Defense | armor, toughness, protection shred resistance, damage absorption |
| Mobility | movement speed, jump count, elytra speed |
| Utility | pickup range, luck, hunger and health maximums |
| Elemental pressure | fire, frost, freeze, flare, radiation, confusion, bleeding |

Reference pages should preserve the exact `yetrealm:*` and Apothic attribute IDs when documenting builds.
