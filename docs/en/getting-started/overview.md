# Yetrealm

Yetrealm is a Minecraft 1.21.1 / NeoForge content and progression mod focused on new realms, bosses, structures, gear stages, Curios accessories, a Skill Tree, configurable world difficulty, and long-term survival goals.

The main route starts with Ancient Soul, Meteorite materials, and the Ancient Portal, then moves into the Ancient Universe. Players explore ancient biomes, abyssal ruins, frozen structures, and dangerous landmarks while upgrading through Meteorite, Ancient Metal, Primordial Alloy, and Plasma gear. Major bosses include Ancient Dragon, Ice Queen, Reaper, and Stargazer.

Beyond combat, Yetrealm adds many supporting systems: Curios slots, a Skill Tree, Cosmic Cube storage, personal backpack-style utilities, food diminishing returns, elite mobs, world-difficulty configuration, and client-side visual features. It is best suited for players who want exploration, gear progression, late-game bosses, modpack difficulty curves, and long survival objectives.
