# Getting Started and Progression

Use this page as the English player route for CurseForge, Modrinth, or a public wiki. It should help a new player understand what to do after installing Yetrealm.

## Recommended Route

| Phase | Goal | Suggested screenshots |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Early preparation | Collect early Curios, mobility tools, and base materials before entering the mod's long-term route. | Item icon sheet, Curios screen, early materials |
| Ancient gateway | Obtain Ancient Soul, activate the Ancient Portal in an ancient city, and enter the Ancient Universe. | Ancient Soul, portal activation, dimension entry |
| Ancient exploration | Explore ancient terrain, abyss/volcanic resources, ruins, and material routes to raise gear strength. | Ancient Universe panorama, abyss map, ores, structures |
| Boss challenges | Defeat major bosses to obtain frost, dragon, abyss, and Reaper-route core materials. | Boss closeups, skill screenshots, loot views |
| Astral entry | Use Astral Keystone progression and the Astral Beacon to open the path into the Astral Realm. | Astral Beacon structure, Astral Realm entry |
| Endgame goals | Push Stargazer, black-hole return, Plasma gear, Cosmic Cube, and other late-game goals. | Stargazer, black hole, Plasma gear, Cosmic Cube UI |
