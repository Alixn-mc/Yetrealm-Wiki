# Astral and Endgame Route

## Route Summary

The endgame route connects Primordial Alloy, Astral Keystone, Astral Beacon activation, the Astral Realm, Stargazer, Star Abyss Core, Cosmic Cube, and Plasma gear.

## Recommended Flow

1. Finish enough Ancient Universe progression to craft Primordial Alloy.
2. Prepare Astral Keystone and the five-layer Astral Beacon route.
3. Enter the Astral Realm with strong mobility and return options.
4. Build toward Stargazer summoning.
5. Use Star Abyss Core and Plasma materials for endgame systems.

!!! warning "Do not rush"
    The Astral route is intended as late-game progression. Entering without mobility, strong gear, and Curios support will make recovery difficult.
