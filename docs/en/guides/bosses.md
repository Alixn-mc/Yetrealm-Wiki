# Boss Guide

## Recommended Order

| Stage | Boss | Goal |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Ancient Universe setup | Ice Queen | Secure frost-route materials and practice mechanic-heavy fights |
| Late Ancient Universe | Ancient Dragon | Obtain dragon-route rewards and late-game materials |
| Reaper route | Reaper | Progress Reaper Scythe, Reaper Seal, and Painful Soul rewards |
| Astral endgame | Stargazer | Obtain Star Abyss Core and push Cosmic Cube / Plasma goals |

## General Preparation

- Build safe portal bases before starting a route.
- Bring mobility Curios and recovery tools.
- Use gear stage upgrades instead of rushing bosses undergeared.
- Keep screenshots of arenas, loot, and mechanics for wiki maintenance.
