# Ancient Universe Resource Route

## Route Summary

1. Find an Ancient City and activate the Ancient Portal with Ancient Soul.
2. Establish a safe Ancient Universe base around the portal exit.
3. Collect Meteorite and Ancient Metal materials.
4. Explore ruins, frozen biomes, abyssal ocean areas, and volcanic regions.
5. Prepare for Ice Queen and Ancient Dragon progression.

## Resource Priorities

| Priority | Resource | Why it matters |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Meteorite | First structured gear stage |
| 2 | Copper Tin / Ancient Metal | Second gear stage and survival strength |
| 3 | Abyssal materials | Underwater route and Deep Abyss gear |
| 4 | Ember and volcanic materials | Astral Keystone and later crafting |
| 5 | Boss drops | Curios and late-game route gates |
