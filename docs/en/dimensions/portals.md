# Portals and Astral Beacon

## Ancient Portal

The Ancient Portal route begins in an Ancient City. Use `Ancient Soul` on the reinforced deepslate portal area to establish a paired route between the Overworld and the Ancient Universe.

| Step | Player action | Result |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Find Ancient City | Reach the central portal frame | Locate the route start |
| Bring Ancient Soul | Use it on the portal area | Activate the Ancient Universe route |
| Build a safe base | Secure both sides of the portal | Stabilize exploration |

## Astral Beacon

The Astral route uses `Astral Keystone` and a five-layer beacon structure. It opens the path into the Astral Realm, where Stargazer progression and late-game materials become available.

!!! tip "Preparation"
    Prepare high mobility, strong armor, Curios slots, and reliable return tools before pushing into Astral progression.
